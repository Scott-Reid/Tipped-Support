Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore my subscription?
    Open Tipped → Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings on your iPhone.
  • My data isn't showing up.
    Ensure you're using the same Apple account and app version. If the issue persists, contact support.
  • How do I report a bug?
    Email us with a description of the issue, your device model, and your iOS version.
